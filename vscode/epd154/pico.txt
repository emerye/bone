  == Hardware connection ==
    EPD    =>    Pico 
  * VCC    ->    3.3 Pin 40
  * GND    ->    GND Pin 38
  * DIN    ->    MOSI SPI0 GP16  Pin 21 
  * CLK    ->    SCLK SPI0 GP18  Pin 24 
  * CS     ->    CS SPIO   GP17  Pin 22 
  * D/C    ->    GP14 Out Pin 19 
  * RES    ->    GP15 Out Pin 20
  * BUSY   ->    GP22 In  Pin 22
