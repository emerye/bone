  == Hardware connection ==
    EPD    =>    Pico 
Pin 1  * VCC    ->    3.3 Pin 36 
Pin 2  * GND    ->    GND Pin 38
Pin 3  * DIN    ->    MOSI SPI0 GP19  Pin 25 
Pin 4  * CLK    ->    SCLK SPI0 GP18  Pin 24 
Pin 5  * CS     ->    CS SPIO   GP17  Pin 22 
Pin 6  * D/C    ->    GP14 Out Pin 19 
Pin 7  * RES    ->    GP15 Out Pin 20
Pin 8  * BUSY   ->    GP22 In  Pin 29
